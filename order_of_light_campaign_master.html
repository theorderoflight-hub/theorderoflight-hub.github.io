01 — The 108 Framework

First you're something.
Then you're nothing.
Now you're everything.

The number 108 is not decorative. In Buddhist tradition, a mala contains exactly 108 beads — each one representing one of the earthly desires that bind the human self to suffering. To count through all 108 is to move from attachment toward release. In yoga, 108 sun salutations mark a threshold — a physical commitment to transformation. In Vedic cosmology, the distance between the Earth and the Sun is approximately 108 times the Sun's diameter. The number describes the distance between the bounded self and the source of all light.

The Order of Light did not choose this number. The number was already there, waiting to be found. It describes, with uncomfortable precision, exactly what the three-EP arc of this campaign does to a listener who travels all the way through it.

"108 is not a concept. It is a coordinate. And we have always known where it points."
— The Conduit, Transmission 003

The Transmission Tracks

Between each EP, a single standalone track is released — existing outside the EP framework entirely. These Transmission Tracks serve as connective tissue, exclusive early drops for The Illuminated, and narrative bridges between the three states. They are not on any EP. They are simply signals.

Transmission Track A · Between 108.I and 108.II
"The Signal Persists"

Released exclusively to The Illuminated 2 weeks before public release. Sonically, this track lives at the threshold between Something and Nothing — the moment just before the self begins to dissolve. Lyrically ambiguous, atmospheric, unresolved. The Illuminated receive it with a personalised transmission note from The Conduit acknowledging them as the first to hear the bridge.

Transmission Track B · Between 108.II and 108.III
"Found Frequency"

The bridge between Nothing and Everything. This track is the pivot point of the entire arc — the moment after dissolution where something new begins to form. Again released exclusively to The Illuminated first, framed as "the signal has located its final frequency." More expansive than Track A, hints at the resolution of EP3 without delivering it.

The 108 in the Marketing

The number 108 should surface gradually across the campaign without explanation. It begins as a detail — a reference in a Sermon, a number on the ordination certificate, perhaps the first run of kits is exactly 108 units. It accumulates meaning slowly. By the time EP3 drops, the audience who has been paying close attention will feel the full weight of it. This is the reward for staying with the Order across the year.

  • First run of ordination kits: 108 units, hand-numbered 001/108 to 108/108
  • The Conduit references "the 108" obliquely in Sermon 002 — never explaining what it means
  • Transmission 003 contains the first direct reference: "108 is not a concept. It is a coordinate."
  • EP1 artwork contains 108 hidden somewhere in the design — discovered by fans, never confirmed by the band
  • The Discord (The Chamber) has exactly 108 founding member slots before general access opens
  • Between EP2 and EP3, post a single image: the number 108 against void black. No caption. No context.

10 — The Conduit

AI Character Brief:
The Conduit

The Conduit is the human face of the Order's transmissions. They are not a band member. They do not exist outside the Order's transmissions. Their identity is deliberately ambiguous — era, origin, and name are all withheld, not by accident but by design. The slight wrongness of an AI-generated character at the edges of its uncanny valley is not a limitation here — it is the aesthetic.

Character Specifications

Apparent Age

Ambiguous — could plausibly be 35 or 55. Neither young nor old. The face should carry the quality of someone who has been awake for a long time without appearing tired.

Gender / Presentation

Androgynous or gently ambiguous. Not aggressively gender-neutral — simply not defined enough to anchor in either direction. This universalises the character and prevents the audience projecting a specific archetype.

Ethnicity / Origin

Deliberately non-specific. No strong regional features that would tie the character to a particular geography. The Order's signal has no nationality.

Wardrobe

A plain, collarless shirt or simple jacket. No logos, no patterns, no era-specific styling. The colour should be neutral — off-white, pale grey, or a very muted tone. Nothing that reads as modern.

Lighting

One warm source from the left, casting the right side of the face into shadow. The ratio should be approximately 3:1 — three parts light, one part shadow. The lit side should be warm amber; the shadow side should be cool and dark. This is consistent across all Sermons — never varies.

The Specific Wrongness

The AI-generated face should be held at slightly too-high resolution in some areas and slightly too-smooth in others. The eyes, in particular, should feel almost but not quite correctly alive. Do not attempt to correct or smooth over these artefacts — they are the point.

HeyGen Generation Prompt

Avatar Creation — HeyGen / Midjourney Reference Image

To generate the base image for The Conduit's visual identity (for use as HeyGen avatar source or Midjourney concept):

"A person of ambiguous age (35–55) and androgynous features, facing slightly off-camera to the right, lit by a single warm amber light source from the left, right side of face in deep shadow. Plain collarless off-white shirt. Non-specific dark background. Serious, calm expression — no smile, no overt emotion, eyes slightly unfocused as if looking at something distant. Film grain. Analogue photography aesthetic. 1980s documentary portrait. Slightly overexposed on the lit side."

Generate 4–6 variations and select the one with the highest degree of pleasant uncanniness — the one that is most compelling and most slightly-wrong simultaneously.

ElevenLabs Voice Design Brief

Voice Parameters — ElevenLabs Custom Voice

Base voice: Select a voice with inherent warmth — not cold or clinical. Baritone or low mezzo range preferred.

Stability: High (0.75–0.85) — the voice should not vary significantly between sentences. Its consistency is part of its authority.

Similarity / Clarity: Medium-high (0.65–0.75) — present and clear, but not hyper-produced.

Speaking rate: Apply a 0.85x speed reduction in post — the Conduit speaks slowly, deliberately, never rushing.

Breathing: Minimal — the Conduit does not sigh, does not rush, does not betray effort. The breaths are controlled and infrequent.

Post-processing: Apply a subtle tape compression (gentle saturation + slight high-frequency roll-off) to make the voice feel like it has been recorded to analogue tape and played back. Not heavy — just enough to remove the digital cleanliness.

Maintaining Consistency

  • Document every HeyGen parameter, seed number, and setting used to generate The Conduit. Save this as a separate locked file — losing this information means losing the character's consistency.
  • The Conduit's appearance does not change between Sermons. Same avatar, same lighting setup, same crop. The only thing that changes is what they say.
  • The Conduit has no name in any public communication. They are referred to only as "the transmission," "the voice," "the signal." If the audience begins to name the character, do not engage with or contradict their speculation.
  • The Conduit never appears outside the Sermons — not in Stories, not in posts, not in any other context. Their presence is confined to the transmissions. This exclusivity preserves the character's authority.
