Built for artists who are building something that lasts.

Signal House LLC handles the business so artists can focus on the work — management, publishing administration, creative strategy, brand development, and label services through our First Broadcast imprint.

We work with a small number of artists at any one time. Not because we have to, but because real development requires attention, not volume. Selective. Independent. Long-term.
