01 — What Signal House Is

Signal House LLC is a management and publishing company. It exists to handle the business side of a music operation — legal structure, publishing administration, artist development strategy, label services through the First Broadcast imprint.

When industry contacts receive a communication from Signal House, they should immediately understand they are dealing with a professional operation. Not a bedroom project. A real company with real processes. That is the only job this brand has to do.

What it is
Management company. Publishing administrator. Label parent. Legal and financial entity for all creative operations.
What it isn't
A record label (that's First Broadcast). An artist (that's OOL). A creative brand. A thing fans interact with.
Primary audience
Booking agents, promoters, sync licensors, PROs, legal contacts, distributors, label A&R, press.
Not for
Fans. The general public. Anyone looking for music or merch — they go to The Order of Light.
02 — Visual Identity

Signal House uses a cool slate-white palette — decisively different from The Order of Light's near-black world and from First Broadcast's contemporary clean-white. The steel blue accent has no warmth, no crimson. Professional authority without personality.

03 — Voice & Tone

Signal House communicates with economy and precision. Every word earns its place. No creative language in a business context. No enthusiasm. No hedging.

Signal House writes like this
Not like this
"We'd like to discuss a booking for Q2. Available dates attached."
"We're SO excited about this opportunity and would love to connect!"
"The rider is attached. Please confirm receipt within 5 business days."
"Just following up to see if you had a chance to look things over!"
"Our rate for this is $X. We can discuss if the budget is different."
"We're totally flexible and open to whatever works best for you!"
04 — Email Standard

All Signal House correspondence uses the same signature format. Consistent across every contact. The name, title, entity, contact. Nothing else.
