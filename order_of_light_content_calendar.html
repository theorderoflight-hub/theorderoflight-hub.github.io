Category Cadence Format Notes
Broadcast Quarterly — 5 total Video 90s–3min Scheduled 24hrs out minimum. Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube simultaneously.
Release 3 EPs — Q1/2, Q2/3, Q4 DSP + social campaign Each EP has a 4-week campaign window: tease, announce, pre-save, release day, sustain.
Social 3–4 posts/week Static image, short video, text Mix: 1 mythology/atmospheric, 1 behind-the-scenes, 1 community/interaction per week. Templates in Social Templates document.
Frequency Weeks 30–33 (4-week series) Single releases + reaction Week 30: OOL Transmission 1. Week 31: collaborator 2. Week 32: collaborator 3. Week 33: reaction content.
Illuminated Monthly exclusive drop Voice memo, demo, update Consistent monthly delivery is non-negotiable. Illuminated members leave if the content stops arriving.
Live From Week 24 onwards Shows, residency, support slots No live shows before the band is rehearsal-ready. First show after full EP 01 campaign is complete.
On social cadence: 3–4 posts per week is the minimum to maintain algorithmic visibility. Quality above quantity — a week with 3 strong posts beats a week with 6 mediocre ones. Never post just to post. If there is nothing worth saying, post the mark on void black and nothing else. That is always sufficient.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–8
Pre-Launch — The world before the music
8 weeks ▾
Wk 1–2
Accounts go live. Instagram, TikTok, YouTube created. The mark on void black. No bio text yet. No music. The presence exists before it explains itself.
Post frequency: 1–2/week. Just the mark. No caption or minimal caption.
Wk 3–4
Atmospheric content begins. Short abstract videos — signal, darkness, the frequency aesthetic. No band members. No music. The world, not the band.
Broadcast 001 in production. Not live yet — this is production week.
Wk 5
Broadcast 001 drops. The first transmission. The mythology's public debut. Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube simultaneously. No press release. No announcement. It simply appears.
Allow 48 hours before any follow-up social post. Let it exist alone first.
Wk 6–7
Post-Broadcast content — atmospheric follow-ups. Short clips from the Broadcast. The mark. Silence posts.
The Illuminated soft launch. Landing page live with waitlist. No active promotion yet — just available for those who find it through the Broadcast.
Wk 8
EP 01 tease begins. First mention of music — not the EP itself, but the fact that there is music coming. Abstract. Cryptic. "Something is being prepared."
EP 01 submitted to Symphonic Distribution this week — minimum 4-week lead time required.
Phase 2 · Weeks 9–20
EP 01 — Something · Campaign and release
12 weeks ▾
Wk 9–10
EP 01 announced. Artwork, track list, release date. Pre-save link live. Press kit sent to target blogs and playlist curators.
The Illuminated officially launches. Founding memberships open (108 cap). Kit fulfilment ordered.
Wk 11
Single 1 from EP 01 released. The most accessible track. Social campaign — static ads, short video clips, lyric posts.
Wk 12–13
Single campaign sustain. Behind-the-scenes content. The Illuminated exclusive: demo version of EP track.
Broadcast 002 in production.
Wk 14
EP 01 release day. All tracks live. Release day social campaign. Illuminated members had 1-week early access — this is the public release.
Broadcast 002 drops. Release day + Broadcast on the same day — the mythology and the music arrive together.
Wk 15–18
EP 01 sustain campaign. Playlist pitching follow-ups. Blog coverage coordination. Social: fan reception content, reaction, atmospheric follow-ups.
Monthly Illuminated drop — voice memo: the story behind an EP 01 track.
First live show conversations begin — venue booking, support slot outreach.
Wk 19–20
Bridge content — between EP 01 and EP 02. Atmospheric, mysterious. Signal of what's coming without announcement.
EP 02 submitted to Symphonic Distribution this week.
Phase 3 · Weeks 21–33
EP 02 — Nothing · Dissolution arc + The Frequency
13 weeks ▾
Wk 21–22
EP 02 announced. The tone shifts — "Nothing" is darker, more uncertain. Artwork reflects this. Social content changes register from EP 01.
Wk 23
Single from EP 02 released.
First live show (if ready). Small venue, Charlotte. The live show as a transmission — production consistent with the visual identity.
Wk 24–25
EP 02 release day.
Broadcast 003 drops. Aligned with EP 02 release.
Wk 26–29
EP 02 sustain. Live show content if shows have happened. Illuminated monthly drop.
The Frequency announcement. Series name, concept, collaborators revealed. The Source has already been sent — collaborators are recording. Announcement goes live Week 29.
Wk 30
The Frequency — Transmission 1 (The Order of Light). OOL's version released as a standalone single. Social campaign for the series launch.
Wk 31
Transmission 2 — Established collaborator. Their version drops. Coordination with their social team. Cross-promotion.
Wk 32
Transmission 3 — Emerging collaborator. Final version drops.
Wk 33
Week 4 — Reaction content. All three versions are live. The Order of Light publishes reaction content: side-by-side comparisons, commentary on the differences. The Illuminated exclusive: an extended commentary.
Phase 4 · Weeks 34–46
EP 03 — Everything · Completion and expansion
13 weeks ▾
Wk 34–35
EP 03 announced. "Everything" — the arc completes. Announcement tone: arrival, not urgency. The campaign has been building to this.
EP 03 submitted to Symphonic Distribution.
Wk 36
Single from EP 03 released.
Wk 38
EP 03 release day.
Broadcast 004 drops.
Wk 39–42
EP 03 sustain. The arc complete — social content can look back across the three EPs as a complete body of work for the first time.
Live shows — with three EPs complete, a proper set list exists. Expand show ambition beyond Charlotte.
Wk 43–46
Illuminated Year End exclusive — a Broadcast made only for founding members. Never published publicly.
Year One retrospective content. The 108 arc from Something to Nothing to Everything — documented.
Phase 5 · Weeks 47–52
Year Two Signal — The first transmission forward
6 weeks ▾
Wk 47–49
Broadcast 005 drops. The final Year One Broadcast — plants the first signal of Year Two. Something unresolved. Something new entering the frequency. No announcement. Just the signal.
Wk 50–52
Year Two pre-production begins quietly. The visual palette shift starts appearing in social content — the deep blue entering at the edges. Audience notices before anything is said.
Year Two record recording and production active. The Year Two campaign document takes over from this point.
