01

Philosophy

The Illuminated is the inner community of The Order of Light. It is designed as a genuine creative community — not a revenue stream disguised as one. The distinction matters: members pay to be closer to the work, not to be marketed at from a shorter distance.

Everything the Illuminated receives — early access, exclusive content, physical objects — should feel like it was made for them specifically. The mythology of the project makes this natural: they are not subscribers, they are members of an order. That framing should be consistent across every touchpoint.

The founding cap of 108 is not arbitrary — it is the mythology's central number, and it makes the founding cohort genuinely finite. Once 108 founding memberships are issued, they cannot be reissued. This is not a marketing trick. It is a commitment to the people who arrived first.

03

Ordination Kit — Contents

Physical kit shipped to founding members only. Each item numbered 1–108. Production brief — not final design.

01
Ordination Certificate
Heavyweight uncoated stock. Numbered in sequence. The Order of Light mark blind-embossed. Member's number hand-written or letterpressed. The founding document.
02
The Mark — Enamel Pin
The eight-pointed geometry in hard enamel. Black base, gold fill. 25mm. A physical version of the mark — the only official merchandise that carries it in three dimensions.
03
The Frequency — Year One Source
A printed copy of The Source — the chart and lyric sheet sent to collaborators. The same object the artists received, printed and numbered. Evidence of the process.
04
The 108 — Print
A4 or letter format. Void black background. The number 108 in DM Sans ExtraLight at extreme scale. Nothing else. The mythology's central number as a physical object.
05
Wax Seal Sticker Sheet
The Order of Light mark reproduced as a sheet of premium foil stickers. The mark in gold, crimson, and parchment. Eight stickers per sheet.
06
The Kit — Outer Packaging
Black rigid mailer or folding box. The mark and member number printed on the exterior. Inside: black tissue, sealed with the wax-impression sticker. The unboxing is part of the mythology.
Production note: source quotes for print and enamel manufacturing before confirming the $108 price point. Minimum order quantities for enamel pins are typically 50–100 units — the 108 cap works with this. Recommend using a print-on-demand certificate printer for the numbered certificates to avoid holding stock. Budget approximately $30–45 per kit all-in at 108 units.

Launch Sequence

Pre-launch
Month −2
Set up membership platform and Discord
Choose platform (Memberful recommended for direct Stripe integration), configure tiers and pricing, build out Discord channels and role gating. Test the full flow end-to-end before any public announcement.
Pre-launch
Month −1
Finalise and manufacture ordination kits
Place manufacturing orders for enamel pins and print materials. Confirm all 108 kit components are ready to ship within two weeks of founding membership close. Do not open founding memberships until kit stock is confirmed.
Launch
EP 01 week
Founding memberships open — 108 only
Announce alongside EP 01. The Illuminated opens simultaneously with the first release. The founding cap is prominently displayed — a live counter if the platform supports it. The mythology framing in all announcement copy: these are the first to receive the signal.
Post-launch
+2 weeks
Kit fulfilment — founding members
Ship all ordination kits within two weeks of founding memberships closing or reaching 108, whichever comes first. Tracking number sent to each member. Document the packing and shipping process for social content — the kits being packed is content.
Ongoing
Monthly
Regular tier and general memberships open permanently
The Signal and The Frequency tiers remain open indefinitely after launch. Founding tier closes once 108 is reached — permanently. Monthly voice memo and exclusive content delivered consistently. Consistency is the foundation of the community's trust.

Platform Recommendation

Recommended
Memberful
Integrates directly with Stripe (lowest fees), allows custom branding, supports Discord role integration natively, and lets you host content directly. No marketplace — members join through your own site. 4.9% + $0.30 per transaction. Most professional option for a project that wants to control its own presentation.
Alternative
Patreon
Higher discoverability, established audience familiarity with the format. 8% platform fee on the Pro plan. Built-in Discord integration. Lower control over branding and presentation — members see the Patreon interface, not yours. Acceptable if launching quickly is the priority.
Not recommended
Bandcamp subscriptions
Limited to music and downloads. No Discord integration, no physical tier support, no community features. Fine as an additional channel but not a primary membership platform.
