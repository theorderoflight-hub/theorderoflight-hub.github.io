01

What This Is

This document is The Source — the only material you receive for The Frequency collaboration. It contains a Nashville Numbers chart and a lyric sheet. Nothing else. No reference recording. No guide vocal. No production direction.

You interpret this entirely in your own voice, in your own world. There are no wrong answers. There is no version we're hoping for. The only requirement is that the harmonic structure in the chart is followed — the chords are the shared foundation. Everything else is yours.

Three artists are receiving this document independently. None of you will hear the other versions until all three are released in the same week. The point of The Frequency is the comparison — three entirely different interpretations of the same signal. Your version should be entirely yours. Resist any temptation to imagine what the others might do.

Read the embargo clause before anything else. It is at the end of this document. Your participation in The Frequency is conditional on your agreement to it. Reply to tristanmatthew@gmail.com to confirm you have read and agreed before you begin recording.
02

The Rules

Simple, few, and non-negotiable.

Do
Follow the chord structure in the Nashville Numbers chart. The harmonic foundation is the shared element — this is what connects all three versions.
Do
Use the lyrics as written. You may omit sections, repeat sections, or reorder them. You may not rewrite them. The words are ours — the interpretation is yours.
Do
Record and produce the track in your own style, in your own environment, with your own people. No instructions on production, instrumentation, tempo, or arrangement.
Don't
Do not share this document, the chart, or the lyrics with anyone outside your immediate recording collaborators until after Week 4 release. This is the embargo clause — details at the end.
Don't
Do not seek out or listen to any other version of this song before your recording is complete. If you encounter another version accidentally, note it and let us know. Your version should be untainted by others.
Note
Credit: the song is credited as written by The Order of Light. Your recording is credited as performed by you. Standard co-interpretation credit — your version is yours to release, license, and own as a master recording. Royalty split documented in the collaboration agreement.
Deadline
Final mixed and mastered audio file due eight weeks before your agreed release date. WAV, 16-bit/44.1kHz minimum. One revision round if required. Contact us before the deadline if you need an extension — surprises are harder to manage than advance notice.
