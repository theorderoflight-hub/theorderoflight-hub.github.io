01 — Identity

firstbroadcast is the label imprint — the name on the record. It faces press, streaming platforms, playlist curators, and the listening public. Where Signal House is cold corporate authority, firstbroadcast is modern editorial confidence. Where The Order of Light is mythological darkness, firstbroadcast is clean and direct.

The name is styled as one word, all lowercase. "first" in light weight, "broadcast" in semibold. Same baseline. The visual weight difference creates a natural emphasis — the transmission is what matters.

05 — Voice

firstbroadcast is editorial but not pretentious. It talks about music concretely — what it does, not what it "is." It doesn't use the mythological language of The Order of Light. It doesn't use the corporate precision of Signal House. It sounds like a label that actually listens to what it releases.

Signal House
"Please find the rider attached. Confirm receipt within 5 business days."
firstbroadcast
The Order of Light's debut EP arrives this spring. Eight songs. No filler. Available everywhere.
The Order of Light
"The signal is coming. The Conduit has been broadcasting. The first transmission arrives."
The distinction: firstbroadcast describes music with editorial precision. It says what something does to the listener, not what mythology surrounds it. Leave the mythology to OOL.
